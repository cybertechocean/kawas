# About KAWA'S

KAWA'S Café is a premium **Halal Certified** specialty coffee house located in the heart of **Nyali, Mombasa**. We are passionate about creating memorable café experiences through handcrafted coffee, signature beverages, freshly prepared desserts, and warm hospitality.

Inspired by the elegance of modern cafés in **Dubai, Doha, and Istanbul**, while embracing the relaxed coastal charm of Mombasa, KAWA'S offers an inviting space for families, friends, professionals, students, tourists, and coffee lovers alike.

Whether you're starting your morning with a perfectly brewed espresso, enjoying an artisan matcha latte, meeting friends over desserts, or working remotely with a cup of specialty coffee, KAWA'S is designed to make every visit memorable.
