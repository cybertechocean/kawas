The Beginning

Where Passion Meets Coffee

KAWA'S Café was born from a deep passion for exceptional coffee and a desire to bring a world-class café experience to Nyali, Mombasa. Inspired by the elegant specialty coffee houses of Dubai, Doha, and Istanbul, we set out to create something truly special for the Kenyan coast.

Our name "KAWA'S" comes from the Swahili word for coffee — "Kahawa" — a nod to our roots and our love for this beautiful beverage that connects people across cultures and generations.

Every element of KAWA'S has been thoughtfully crafted: from our premium espresso machine and carefully sourced coffee beans, to our artisan desserts and the warm, welcoming atmosphere of our café space.
