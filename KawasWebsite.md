## Premium Halal Café Experience
**Project:** kawas.co.ke
**Client:** Nash
**Location:** Mount Kenya Road, Nyali, Mombasa, Kenya

---

# Vision

The KAWA'S website should **not** feel like a typical Kenyan café website.

Instead, it should feel like walking into a premium café in:

- 🇦🇪 Dubai
- 🇶🇦 Doha
- 🇹🇷 Istanbul

...while still embracing the relaxed coastal atmosphere of **Nyali, Mombasa**.

The design should communicate:

- Premium Quality
- Halal Excellence
- Warm Hospitality
- Coastal Luxury
- Modern Muslim Lifestyle
- Instagram-Worthy Experiences

The overall atmosphere should resemble luxury cafés such as % Arabica, Bateel Café, EL&N, or premium Turkish coffee houses rather than fast-food restaurants or generic coffee chains.

# Muslim-Friendly UX

The website should naturally reflect halal values without appearing overly religious.

### Trust & Transparency

Prominently display:

- 🟢 Halal Certified
- 🟢 100% Alcohol-Free
- 🟢 Fresh Ingredients
- 🟢 Family Friendly

### Prayer-Friendly Features

Optional:

- Live prayer times for Nyali (via Aladhan API)
- Ramadan mode with Suhoor/Iftar hours
- Eid-themed homepage banners
- Admin-controlled holiday announcements

### Language

Use hospitality-focused messaging such as:

- Crafted With Care
- Freshly Prepared
- Served With Excellence
- Made For Sharing
- Welcome To Our Table

Avoid nightlife or bar-related language.

### Mocktail Positioning

Instead of emphasizing "Mojitos," clearly label the category as:

> **Alcohol-Free Mojitos (Mocktails)**

This eliminates ambiguity and reinforces halal positioning.

# Final Creative Direction

KAWA'S should feel like a destination, not just a café.

Every interaction—from the warm espresso palette and editorial typography to the immersive photography, elegant micro-animations, and halal-first messaging—should reinforce the feeling of stepping into a premium café in Dubai, Doha, or Istanbul, while celebrating the relaxed elegance of Nyali, Mombasa.

The experience should be modern, luxurious, welcoming, and unmistakably **Halal Certified**, inspiring confidence for Muslim families and delighting every visitor seeking exceptional coffee, desserts, and hospitality.
